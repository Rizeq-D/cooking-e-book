The Ingredients:

8 pilled potato
15 tabletsBlack olives (quantity as desired)
3 clovesMashed garlic
salt
A corn of sugar
MKVinegar as desired
Corn cumin corn red hot pepper powder
2To 3 tbsp olive oil
2 mkChopped coriander (leaves only)

Steps:

Step 1
We peel the potatoes, cut them into circles,
and boil them in water for about 10 to 15 minutes until they become tender.
Drain them from the water well and leave them to cool well.

Step 2
Add garlic, cumin, hot pepper, vinegar, salt, olive oil and mix well.

Step 3
Add olives and coriander and bon appetite.
